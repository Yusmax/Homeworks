AK47

The AK-47, also known as the Kalashnikov, is a renowned assault rifle with a rich history. Designed by Mikhail Kalashnikov, this firearm is celebrated for its exceptional reliability and widespread use worldwide. Its simple, rugged design has made it an icon of military hardware. The AK-47 fires the 7.62x39mm cartridge and is distinguished by its distinctive curved magazine. With a selective-fire capability, it can operate in both semi-automatic and automatic modes. The AK-47 has played a significant role in various conflicts and is favored for its durability and ease of use. It continues to be a symbol of military might and has left an indelible mark on the world of firearms.
